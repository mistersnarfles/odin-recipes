Breakfast Burrito

Home

This is a breakfast burrito I have been making for a long time. Very easy to make with few ingredients.

Ingredients (serves 5 people)

  • 15 eggs
  • 5 tortillas
  • 3/4 of a frozen bag of your choice of potatoes
  • 1 pack of preferred bacon
  • 1 pack of preferred sausage
  • 1/2 lb of medium cheddar
  • Devin's Dank Salsa

Steps

  1. Shred cheese and put in fridge
  2. Put forzen potatoes in air fryer using instrucitons on bag
  3. Chop bacon and sausage into 1/2 inch pieces or smaller
  4. Throw bacon and sausage into skillet and cook all the way through. I like to cook bacon first then sausage.
  5. While meats are cooking, I like to crack all of the eggs in a bowl to prepare for scrambling.
  6. Remove bacon and sausage and set inside oven or microwave so it keeps warm
  7. Remove some of the grease, but leave some for the eggs
  8. Add bowl of scrambled eggs and cook to your desired doneness or fully scrambled
  9. Heat up tortillas until soft and warm
  10. Place one tortilla on a plate and assemble the burrito using all ingredients prepared
  11. Repeat for other 4 burritos

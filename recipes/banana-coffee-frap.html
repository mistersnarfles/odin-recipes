This banana coffee is for all the banana coffee lovers out there. Not too sweet and easy to prepare. All you need are the ingredients listed below and a small blender.

Ingredients (makes 1 frap)

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 12 oz of your preferred cold brew
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Splash of half and half
  • 1 cup of ice

Steps

  1. Cut banana into small slices and put into blender
  2. Add honey
  3. Add coffee
  4. Add half and half
  5. Add ice
  6. Blend until your preferred texture and enjoy
